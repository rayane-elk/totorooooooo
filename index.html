Informations

Story

Okabe Rintarou, a university student who refers to himself as Crazy Mad Scientist Hououin Kyouma and his lab's members work on a microwave device that can transfer messages to the past. Without getting captured, they should get it working in order to beat the evil organization, SERN and stop their evil plans.

Main Character

Rintaro Okabe (岡部 倫太郎, Okabe Rintarō?), often nicknamed Okarin (オカリン) or Hououin Kyouma (鳳凰院凶真, Hououin Kyōma), is a self-proclaimed mad scientist and the main protagonist of the Steins;Gate series.

Awards

In 2011, Steins;Gate was part of the Jury Selections of the 15th Japan Media Arts Festival in the Animation category.it won a Newtype Anime Award for the best male anime character of the year, for Rintaro Okabe.Steins;Gate was nominated for the Seiun Award in the Best Media category in 2012.The series is considered as one of the best anime series of the 2010s.IGN listed Steins;Gate among the best anime series of the 2010s.Crunchyroll also listed it in their "Top 100 best anime of the 2010s".
